C-BUR: Curriculum-Based Undergraduate Research

The traditional approach to teaching science courses usually involves focusing on already well-understood models and theories. This approach is similar to trying to teach baseball by covering all the rules but never letting people play the game. With C-BUR, my aim is to let students "play the game" of science.

I started C-BUR because students should have a chance to taste that same thrill of discovering something new that has inspired so many working scientists. Also, I believe that by participating in authentic research activities, students improve their critical thinking skills, develop skills useful for solving real world problems, and obtain a deeper understanding about scientific thinking.
