NevadaSAT: Nevada Student Satellite Program

UNR faculty Jeffrey LaComb, Eric Wang, and I started the Nevada Student Satellite Program to provide students in Nevada with hands-on training opportunities relevant for careers in aerospace science and technology.

Through NevadaSAT we offer a wide range of exciting and inspiring programs for students, including projects in High-Altitude Ballooning, High-Powered Rocketry, and Robotics. In addition to providing hands on training, these dramatic programs also serve to recruit students into STEM careers.

Be sure to check out the photo-galleries of past missions.
