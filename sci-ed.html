Science Education

I am involved in many ongoing collaborations at colleges around the U.S. to develop better assessment tools and active-learning curricula for intro astronomy and physics courses.

I am an advocate for active learning. The evidence in support for active learning has been compelling for over two decades now, and only becomes more persuasive as additional research is published.

Through active learning curricula students achieve significant and lasting learning gains, that traditional lecture, meanwhile, has consistently failed to produce. As an educator, my organizing maxim is "telling is not teaching, and listening is not learning."
